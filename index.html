University Climbing Club

University Climbing Club (UCC) is founded in 1978 by a group of students who are enthusiastic in mountain climbing. It's been four decades since then and the club has won many prices on the regional level. Whether you're a casual climber or a professional one, our club can provide all the facilities and equipments that will suit your need. Everyone is welcome to join and have fun.
